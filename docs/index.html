App Guide

Designed to respect your time.

AntPod is built to be less addictive, not more — inspired by Humane by Design.

No notifications — AntPod never interrupts you
No autoplay — you choose what's next; nothing plays on its own
Episodes load once — on app startup only, not constantly refreshing
No algorithm — no content pushed at you to keep you scrolling

Come join the anthill.
