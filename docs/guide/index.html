AntPod

Guide

Everything from first launch to fine-tuning your feed.

Subscribe

First launch opens Discover automatically. Tap Discover in the toolbar any time to get back.

1

Type a podcast name to search the PodcastIndex catalogue, or paste an RSS URL directly.

2

Tap any result to open the podcast and browse its full episode list. You can play and download episodes right away without subscribing.

3

Tap Subscribe in the podcast header. The feed refreshes in the background and episodes appear immediately.

The Trending and Suggestions tabs surface new podcasts based on what's popular or what matches your existing subscriptions.

To unsubscribe, open the podcast and tap the Unsubscribe button (red minus circle) in the podcast header. A confirmation dialog appears before anything is removed.

Feed

All subscriptions in one chronological list. Filter chips at the top narrow it down.

ChipShows
NewUnplayed episodes — on by default
PlayingStarted but not finished, floated to top
DownloadedSaved to device only
ListenedCompleted episodes
PodcastsSwitch to cover grid; tap any cover to open that show
A–Z / Oldest / RandomSort order, combinable with any filter above

Tap Filter in the toolbar to collapse or expand the chip bar. A dot signals active filters.

Tap Search in the toolbar to search by title or podcast name. No results? A button lets you search PodcastIndex without leaving the screen.

Tap any episode cover to jump to that podcast's episode list. Use the back button to return to the feed.

Pull down to refresh all subscriptions.

Playback

Tap the play button on any episode. A sheet slides up with the full player; a mini player stays pinned at the bottom everywhere else.

Control
ChaptersListed above the timeline when available; cover art updates per chapter
TimelineDrag to scrub anywhere in the episode
Skip back / forward10 s and 30 s by default — long-press either button to set your own value
SpeedTap to cycle 1× → 1.5× → 2× → 0.8×
ShareGenerates an antpod.eu/open?… link — anyone can tap it to open the same episode

Audio continues in the background with lock-screen and notification controls. The last-played episode restores on next launch at the exact same position.

Downloads

Tap the Download icon on any episode to save it locally, or swipe left. A progress ring fills while downloading; tap the icon again to cancel.

Once downloaded, playback uses the local file — no streaming. The file is deleted automatically when the episode finishes, or when you pause within the last minute.

To delete manually: swipe left → Delete download, or long-press the tile → context menu.

Sharing & deep links

Every share action — episode or podcast — produces an antpod.eu/open?… URL. Opening it on a device with AntPod installed jumps directly to the podcast or loads the episode into the player. On other devices it opens the website.

Share a podcast from its header; share an episode from the player sheet or episode action menu.
